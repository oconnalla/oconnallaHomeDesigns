Originally it was just a college girl's love for crafts.

Like most stories, my dream started small. I began my crafting journey in my first college apartment. I was on a budget, but I wanted a home I could feel proud of. I began playing with all sorts of materials, from scrap book pages, to chiffon fabric. I didn't know I had a talent for decorating when I first started, but soon I was being asked for tips and tricks by my classmates on how to improve their dorms. I began to wonder if I could make something more out of this creative spark. Fast forward a few years, and my crafting knowlege had expanded as well as my experience.

→It started with small simple creations that were family holiday traditions


→I began to expand my abilites with hours and hours of practice, and a lot of hot glue burns.

I love the feeling of turning a house into a home

I have a passion for creating decorations a person can be proud to have in their home. Home is where the heart is. A homes is a place for making memories and sharing lifelong experiences. My goal with each piece I create is that when brought into an environment, it will make the place feel more like an individual home. I do my best to put quality and care into every item I create. My rule is, if I wouldn't have it at my home, I won't sell it. My my heart to yours, I hope you enjoy every creation you take with you.
